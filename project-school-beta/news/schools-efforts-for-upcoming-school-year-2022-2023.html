School's efforts for upcoming school year 2022-2023

August 22, 2022 | by DepEd Tayo Silangan National High School -Rizal

As the school year 2022-2023 approach, Silangan National High School launched it’s Brigada Eskwela. One of it’s objectives is to refurbish or upgrade it’s current materials and equipment for the upcoming students to use. The brigade ranges from cleaning the classrooms, repairing the chairs, and repainting the classrooms when necessary.

With the help of the parents, students, teachers, and local government, the brigade eskwela 2022 is a success. Without the contribution of everyone, it will be a challenge of the school to prepare it’s facilities for the upcoming school year.

Since we’re still in the middle of the pandemic, there are some things changed or replaced in order to protect everyone from COVID-19 infection. New disinfection areas such as hand wash and alcohol spray are scattered around the school to keep the regular disinfection. There is also temperature checkpoint on all entry points of the school in order to identify students with abnormal temperatures. And most importantly, our isolation area is ready for service. Isolation area is one of the most crucial part of the school, as it isolates students with probable symptoms of COVID-19 and to prevent future infections.
