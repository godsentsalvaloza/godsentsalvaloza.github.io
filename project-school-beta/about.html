About

SILANGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL was put-up through the effort of Mrs. Carolina D. Tumuran, then a Barangay Captain and PTA officer of Silangan Elementary School who filed a “Parents Resolution” requesting a parcel of land of the RSBS/AFP (Retirement and Benefit System of the Armed Forces of the Philippines) be donated to the said school. It was once an annex school of San Mateo National High School under Miss Juana m. garovillas, Principal In-charge from 1994-1997 with the coordination of the DECS officials. The first operation of this school was housed at the Old Health Center of the AFP Village, a small makeshift building located incidentally in the lot area and lasted for four years from 1994-1997. The first two years of operation was handled by MRS. Zenaida D. Corpuz a lone teacher residing at the AFP Village, which was an extension position in the plantilla of San Mateo National High School then MR. Remegio B. Olesco was locally designated O.I.C. at the start from 1994-1997. On June 23,1998, he was appointed and officially designated Teacher In-Charge by DECS Regional Office and Division Office under Special Order No.30 series of 1998 up to November 2003. Mr Ricardo Vergara served as school head from 2003 until 2013.

Silangan National High School is presently headed by Dr. Rebecca Averion.
